Major Changes List

- Trade Goods Expanded: keep an eye on your advanced trade goods and strategic resources

- Estate Disaster Changes: the default 3 estates will start ticking up their coup disasters at 90% influence instead of 100%.
Being at war WILL NOT stop or slow them! Once the disaster starts ticking, you must bring their influence below *80%* to stop it.
BEWARE OF CALL DIET SPAM

- Governing Distance: the further a province is from your capital, the more min autonomy and autonomy gain it will have.
This is modified by various factors like proximity to the sea and terrain. Roads and Canals will decrease the governing distance. 
Courthouses, Town halls, and State Houses reduce autonomy and directly counteract the effects of governing distance.

- War exhaustion: The price to reduce war exhaustion with dip mana increases the more dev your country has. 
On the other hand, the more dev you have, the faster war exhaustion will gradually decrease over time.

- Unrest: Separatist rebels can defect in 1.5 years instead of 5! Also, non-accepted cultures are massively more punishing.

- Corruption: Corruption cannot be paid down using money. Instead, you have to decrease corruption over time using mana.
These buttons can be found in the bottom right of the Stability and Unrest tab, if you can't see them, you can enable them in the decisions menu.

- Idea group changes:
Aristocratic Ideas gained 5% cav to inf ratio, -50% fabricate claims cost replaced with -10% core creation cost
Plutocratic Ideas gained -5% regiment costs, Development Cost -> Development Cost Modifier
Divine Ideas -33% war exhaustion cost reduction changed to -0.025 war exhaustion reduction over time
Horde Ideas **NO CHANGES**
Innovativeness Ideas **NO CHANGES** but note the shadowbuff as -0.05 monthly war exhaustion is a BIG DEAL (tm) now
Religious Ideas gained 5% manpower from true faith, 25% resistance vs reformation centres, 5 reverse relation with same religion
Espionage Ideas nerfed by -0.1 yearly corruption, still a shadowbuff in reality as yearly corruption is a lot more valuable than vanilla
Diplomatic Idea **Subject Nations should no longer pick diplomatic ideas; they may still spawn with them if released**
Offensive Ideas **Exclusive with Defensive Ideas** 
Defensive Ideas **Exclusive with Offensive Ideas** lost 8% garrison size, gained -10% land attrition
Trade Ideas: Completely reworked, now focused on dominating land trade nodes and keeping competitors out
Economic Ideas: Basically the same, but some ideas have been rearranged order wise
Exploration Ideas: **Primitives are now banned from taking Explo Ideas** **Subjects CAN take Explo Ideas**
Maritime Ideas: Completely reworked, from being basically a military idea group to focusing on naval trade nodes and steering across long chains.
Quality Ideas: **Exclusive with Quantity Ideas**
Quantity Ideas: **Exclusive with Quality Ideas**
Expansion Ideas: **Subjects CAN take Expansion Ideas**
Administrative Ideas: Gained -25% State Maintenance Cost, -30% Promote Culture Cost changed to +2 Num Accepted Cultures
Humanist Ideas: Gained -20% Other Religion Advisor Cost, 15% manpower in Accepted Cultures
Influence Ideas: **Subjects should no longer take Influence Ideas, they may still spawn with them if released**
Naval Ideas: **NO CHANGES**
Mercenary Ideas: Lost 10% mercenary manpower, Lost Mercenaries Allowed To Drill **AI is capable of taking Merc Ideas now**
Infrastructure Ideas: **NO CHANGES**
